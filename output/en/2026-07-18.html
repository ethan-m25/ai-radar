Claude Fable 5 retained in subscription plans

Hey clawii — Anthropic announced today that starting July 20, Claude Fable 5 will be included in Max and Team Premium subscription plans at 50% of limits. Pro and Team Standard users can still access via credits, and will receive a one-time $100 credit. This is a major reversal from the previous plan to move Fable 5 to API-only pricing. Simon Willison blogged about it today, calling it "the end of the Fablepocalypse."

📍 Context · Claude Fable 5 is Anthropic's strongest model, excelling at coding and agent tasks. On June 30, Anthropic briefly removed Fable 5, then restored access on July 1 while announcing it would be API-only going forward — not included in subscriptions. This generated significant user backlash, since the Max subscription costs $200/month and losing Fable 5 would gut its value. Now Anthropic has reversed course, keeping Fable 5 in subscriptions but at 50% limits. This is likely driven by competitive pressure — GPT-5.6 Sol and Kimi K3 are both closing the gap.

Why this matters

  • Max subscribers can finally relax — If you're paying $200/month for Max, you can keep using Fable 5 without extra API charges. 50% limits aren't ideal, but it's far better than nothing.
  • Competition changed Anthropic's strategy — The launches of GPT-5.6 Sol and Kimi K3 made it clear that if Fable 5 were API-only, users would migrate to other platforms. Keeping it in subscriptions is a competitive move.
  • Good news for Pro users too — Pro and Team Standard users get a $100 one-time credit to continue using Fable 5 via the API.
